    Instructions

    1. To start the stopwatch click to middle orange button.
    2. To stop the timer you can double click the start button or just stop the time by clicking button left to the start button.
    3. To reset the timer you can click to reset button which is on right side of the start button.
    4. To record laps , you can click to Record lap button which will show you laps.
    5. To close this instruction guide , double click to Instructions button.
